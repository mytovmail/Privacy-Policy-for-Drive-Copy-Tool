Privacy Policy for Drive Copy Tool

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Drive Copy Tool extension ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to the information.

1. Introduction

Drive Copy Tool is a productivity extension designed to help users copy Google Drive files directly from a side panel interface. The extension interacts with your Google Drive account solely to perform the actions you initiate (e.g., copying files).

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. The extension operates entirely on your device and communicates directly with Google's official APIs.

a. Google User Data

To function, the extension requires access to your Google Drive via OAuth2 authentication. This access is used strictly for:

  • Reading the list of files you select in the active browser tab.
  • Copying the selected files to your Google Drive storage.
  • Creating a specific destination folder (e.g., "My Copies") within your Drive to organize copied files.

b. Usage Data

We do not track, log, or analyze your usage behavior. All operations are performed locally within your browser session.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information accessed (file IDs, filenames, folder structures) is used only for the purpose of executing the file copy commands you request.

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to outside parties.
  • No Advertising: The extension does not display ads and does not use your data for advertising purposes.
  • Limited Use Disclosure: Drive Copy Tool's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Permissions Justification

The extension requests the following permissions to function: identity, activeTab, scripting, sidePanel, and access to Google Drive APIs.

5. Data Security

Since the extension does not maintain its own servers, your data remains within the Google ecosystem. We use secure, industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocol for authentication.
